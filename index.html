FROM PASSION TO PURPOSE

DRIP was founded in 2021 by two friends who were tired of choosing between quality and affordability in Indian streetwear. We set out to build a brand that proves premium doesn't have to mean expensive.

Every piece in our collection is designed in-house, crafted from ethically sourced materials, and manufactured in India. We believe in slow fashion — fewer, better pieces that last.

Today, we're a community of 12,000+ customers across India who share our belief: that what you wear is a statement of who you are.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact us for support. This includes name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to customer service requests
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our products and website

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card details on our servers.

Shipping Policy

We ship all orders within 24–48 hours of confirmation. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Express delivery is available at checkout. Free shipping on all orders above ₹999.

Return & Exchange Policy

We offer hassle-free returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. Initiate returns via email or WhatsApp. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or policy questions, contact us at privacy@dripclothing.in or call +91 98765 43210.
