Privacy & Policies

Last updated: March 2025

Return & Exchange Policy

Hassle-free returns within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, in original packaging. WhatsApp +91 93800 13093. Refunds within 5–7 business days.

Shipping Policy

Orders processed and shipped within 24–48 hours. Standard delivery 3–7 business days. Free shipping above ₹599. Tracking sent via SMS and WhatsApp.

Privacy Policy

We collect your name, contact, and address only to fulfill orders. We never sell your data. Payments processed securely by Razorpay — we never store card details.

Sizing Information

All sizes are age-based (2Y, 3Y etc.). If between sizes, size up for comfort. All clothing is pre-washed with minimal shrinkage expected.
