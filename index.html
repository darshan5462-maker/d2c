Our Story 💛

Born from a mother's love for her children, Waa Kids Closet is on a mission to dress every little one in style and comfort.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Founded 2021

From a Mom's Wish to Your Little One's Wardrobe

Waa Kids Closet started as a small WhatsApp business in 2021, born out of a simple frustration: why is it so hard to find cute, affordable, AND safe kids clothing in India?

We started small — just a few designs shared with friends and family. The response was overwhelming. Today, we're a growing brand trusted by over 8,000 families across India.

Every piece we sell is chosen with one question in mind: would we dress our own children in this?

Privacy & Policies

Last updated: March 2025

Return & Exchange Policy

We offer hassle-free returns within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. To initiate a return, simply WhatsApp us at +91 93800 13093 with your order ID and reason. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Shipping Policy

We ship across all of India. Orders are processed and shipped within 24–48 hours. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Free shipping on orders above ₹599. Tracking details will be sent via SMS and WhatsApp.

Privacy Policy

We collect your name, contact details, and address solely to fulfill your orders. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Payments are processed securely by Razorpay and we never store your card details. By shopping with us, you agree to our data practices.

Sizing Information

  • All our sizes are age-based (2Y, 3Y, 4Y etc.) — refer to the size guide on each product
  • If your child is between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort
  • All our clothing is pre-washed and minimal shrinkage is expected

Contact Us

For any queries: WhatsApp +91 93800 13093 or email hello@waakidscloset.in
