Our Story 💛

Born from a mother's love for her children, Waa Kids Closet is on a mission to dress every little one in style and comfort.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Founded 2021

From a Mom's Wish to Your Little One's Wardrobe

Waa Kids Closet started as a small WhatsApp business in 2021, born out of a simple frustration: why is it so hard to find cute, affordable, AND safe kids clothing in India?

Today, we're a growing brand trusted by over 8,000 families across India. Every piece we sell is chosen with one question in mind: would we dress our own children in this?

Privacy & Policies

Last updated: March 2025

Return & Exchange Policy

We offer hassle-free returns within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. WhatsApp us at +91 93800 13093. Refunds processed within 5–7 business days.

Shipping Policy

Orders are processed and shipped within 24–48 hours. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Free shipping on orders above ₹599. Tracking details sent via SMS and WhatsApp.

Privacy Policy

We collect your name, contact details, and address solely to fulfill your orders. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Payments are processed securely by Razorpay.
